EnglishUP GO — account deletion

Account deletion request · Green Monster Media

How to request deletion

  1. Email englishupgosupport@gmail.com with the subject line Delete my EnglishUP GO account.
  2. Include the email address linked to your in-app account (the one you used to register or sign in with Google).
  3. We will confirm your request and delete server-side data within 30 days.

Request deletion by email

What we delete (server)

  • Account (email, nickname, sign-in identifiers)
  • Authentication tokens
  • Aggregated usage analytics (for signed-in users)
  • Diamond balance change logs (fraud prevention)
  • Cloud-synced progress (if you had signed in and synced)

What stays on your device

Local progress, favorites, and settings on your phone are removed only if you uninstall the app or clear its data in Android settings. We do not control .enup backup files stored on your device — delete those yourself if needed.

What may be kept longer

Minimal security logs (without learning content) may be retained for up to 90 days only when required for abuse investigation or legal compliance.

If you never signed in

If you did not create an account or sign in, we do not hold personal data on our servers. Uninstall the app or clear app data in Android settings.
